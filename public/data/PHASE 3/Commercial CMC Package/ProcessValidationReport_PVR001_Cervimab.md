**1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE**

This Process Validation Report (PVR-001) documents the execution and
results of the Process Performance Qualification (PPQ) for the
commercial manufacturing process of cervimab drug substance at Acme
Biologics Manufacturing Inc. This report fulfills Stage 2 of the
three-stage process validation lifecycle as defined in the FDA Guidance
for Industry: Process Validation: General Principles and Practices
(January 2011).

The PPQ demonstrates that the commercial-scale manufacturing process
consistently produces cervimab drug substance of predefined quality,
consistent with results from process development and Stage 1 (process
design) activities documented in the Process Design Report (PDR-001) and
Process Characterization Report (PCR-001).

The scope of this report encompasses the following process operations
performed at the commercial scale (2,000 L bioreactor):

- Cell banking through 2,000 L fed-batch production bioreactor

- Harvest, clarification, and purification (Protein A, Low pH VI, CEX,
  AEX, nanofiltration)

- UF/DF concentration and formulation

- Bulk drug substance sterile filtration, fill, and storage

Stage 3 (Continued Process Verification, CPV) is described in the CPV
Protocol (CPVP-001) and is ongoing.

**3. PPQ DESIGN AND STRATEGY**

**3.1 PPQ Strategy Overview**

The PPQ strategy was developed based on a comprehensive process
characterization program (PCR-001) that identified Critical Quality
Attributes (CQAs), Critical Process Parameters (CPPs), and their proven
acceptable ranges (PARs). The PPQ protocol (PVPR-001) defined:

- Number of PPQ batches: 3 consecutive commercial-scale batches
  (consistent with FDA guidance and industry precedent for biologics)

- Acceptance criteria: predefined, scientifically justified, based on
  process characterization data and Phase 3 clinical batch historical
  data

- Sampling and testing plan: enhanced testing at critical steps; full
  release testing of bulk DS

- Statistical approach: process capability (Ppk) analysis where
  applicable; graphical trend analysis

- Predetermined success criteria per ICH Q8(R2) design space

**6. CONCLUSIONS AND PPQ SUMMARY STATEMENT**

All three PPQ batches (CVM-DS-PPQ-V01, V02, V03) met all predefined
acceptance criteria as defined in the PPQ Protocol (PVPR-001). The
commercial-scale manufacturing process for cervimab drug substance at
Acme Biologics Manufacturing Inc. is validated and capable of
reproducibly producing drug substance that meets all quality
specifications.

**Key conclusions:**

- All CQA acceptance criteria met across all three PPQ batches

- Process capability (Ppk) \>= 1.67 demonstrated for all quantitative
  CQAs with sufficient data (n \>= 9)

- Process robustness confirmed across defined CPP ranges; no
  out-of-specification results

- Viral clearance \>= 4 log10 demonstrated for all relevant virus
  classes per ICH Q5A(R2)

- Minor deviations observed during PPQ; none impacted product quality;
  all formally closed

- Stage 3 Continued Process Verification (CPV) program (CPVP-001) is in
  place and ongoing

This report, together with the Analytical Method Validation Reports and
Process Characterization Report (PCR-001), supports the regulatory
submission for cervimab BLA in the indication of dMMR/MSI-H metastatic
colorectal cancer.
