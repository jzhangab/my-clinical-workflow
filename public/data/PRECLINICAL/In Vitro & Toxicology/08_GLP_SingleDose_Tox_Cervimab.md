**2.2 Observations and Evaluations**

Clinical observations: twice daily. Body weight: prestudy, Days 1, 2, 8,
16. Ophthalmology: prestudy and Day 15. ECG (lead II): prestudy and Day
2. Blood pressure (Doppler): prestudy and Day 2. Hematology, serum
chemistry, coagulation, urinalysis: prestudy, Day 2, Day 16 (recovery).
Immunophenotyping (CD4+, CD8+, CD20+, NK by flow cytometry): prestudy
and Day 2. Toxicokinetics: 0.25, 1, 4, 24, 48, 96, 168h post-dose (Days
1--8). ADA: prestudy, Days 2 and 16. Necropsy: complete gross pathology;
organ weights; full histopathology (\>40 tissues, H&E + special stains
as indicated).

**4. Clinical Observations and Mortality**

No mortality or moribundity. No test article-related clinical
observations at 30 mg/kg. At 100 mg/kg: 2/4 animals showed transient
mild emesis (once each, Day 1 within 4h of infusion end; resolved
spontaneously). No acute infusion reactions (pyrexia, hypotension,
bronchospasm). Body weight: no clinically significant changes at any
dose level (±5% vs predose).

**6. Histopathology**

Gross pathology: No macroscopic findings attributed to test article at
either dose level. Organ weights: no statistically significant changes
at 30 mg/kg. At 100 mg/kg: spleen weight increased 18% in males, 14% in
females (p\<0.05); consistent with lymphoid hyperplasia secondary to
PD-1 pharmacology. Fully reversible at Day 16 recovery necropsy.
