*ADA = Anti-Drug Antibody (ECL assay; confirmatory titer ≥10). ADA
positive rate increases with dose level and study duration ---
consistent with immunogenic response to human protein in non-human
primates. Note: ADA positive at 30 mg/kg with reduced Cmax/AUC at Cycle
7 (0.87× vs C1) indicates ADA-enhanced clearance. This is expected NHP
immunogenicity; ADA rates in NHP do NOT predict human immunogenicity
(pembrolizumab: high ADA in NHP, \~1% in humans). ADA did not cause
adverse toxicological effects in this study (no ADA-mediated immune
complex disease was observed).*
