**1. Introduction**

This report presents the complete tabulated plasma (serum)
concentration-time dataset for Cervimab across all nonclinical and Phase
1 studies. Data are organized by species, study, dose level, and nominal
sampling timepoint. All concentration data were generated using
validated bioanalytical methods (anti-Cervimab capture ELISA or
LC-MS/MS) per ICH M10 Bioanalytical Method Validation (2022). The
dataset supports NCA (MOS-PKT-2022-004) and popPK modeling
(MOS-PKPD-2022-002). Comparison to published PK profiles of structurally
similar anti-PD-1 antibodies ^\[1\]^, ^\[2\]^, ^\[3\]^ provides
translational context.

**2. Bioanalytical Methods**

**2.1 Anti-Cervimab ELISA**

Capture antibody: rabbit anti-Cervimab Fab polyclonal (MOS-Bioanalytics
Lot BAP-2021-014; 2 µg/mL coating); Detection antibody: anti-human IgG4
Fc-HRP (Southern Biotech 9190-05; 1:2000). LLOQ: 5 ng/mL (cynomolgus
monkey serum) and 0.5 µg/mL (human plasma --- re-calibrated for higher
therapeutic concentrations). Calibration range: 5--2,000 ng/mL (cyno);
0.5--500 µg/mL (human). Matrix: serum (cyno) / EDTA plasma (human).
Selectivity: no cross-reactivity with endogenous IgG4 (tested at 20
mg/mL endogenous IgG). Validated per ICH M10 (accuracy ±15%, CV ≤15%;
±20%/≤20% at LLOQ).

**3. Cynomolgus Monkey --- Single-Dose PK (Non-GLP, Study
MOS-PK-2021-004)**

Serum samples were collected from 18 cynomolgus monkeys (n=3M+3F per
dose group: 1, 5, 20 mg/kg IV over 60 min) at pre-dose and 0.25, 1, 4,
24, 48, 96, 168, 336, 504, 672h post-dose. Serum concentration data are
presented as mean ± SD. All data consistent with published anti-PD-1
antibody PK profiles in cynomolgus monkeys: JS-001 (toripalimab) CL
0.29--0.34 mL/h/kg ^\[3\]^; finotonlimab t½ \~206h (\~8.6 days) at 5
mg/kg ^\[1\]^; P1801 dose-dependent linear PK at 1--75 mg/kg ^\[2\]^.
