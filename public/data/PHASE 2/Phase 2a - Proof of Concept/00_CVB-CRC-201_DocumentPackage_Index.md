**STUDY AT A GLANCE**

  --------------------- ---------------------------------------------
  **Drug**              Cervimab --- Fully Humanized IgG4 Anti-PD-1
                        Monoclonal Antibody (S228P stabilized)

  **Dose / Schedule**   200 mg IV every 3 weeks (Q3W); up to 32
                        cycles (\~24 months)

  **Indication**        Previously treated dMMR/MSI-H Metastatic
                        Colorectal Cancer

  **Study Phase**       Phase 2a

  **Design**            Open-label, single-arm, multicenter; Simon
                        Optimal 2-stage design

  **Sample Size**       40 evaluable (Stage 1: N=19; Stage 2: N=21)

  **Primary Endpoint**  ORR by BICR per RECIST v1.1; reject H0
                        (ORR\<=20%) if \>=13 responses in 40 patients

  **Key Secondary**     DoR, PFS, OS, DCR, Safety (CTCAE v5.0)

  **Key Inclusion**     dMMR/MSI-H confirmed (IHC or molecular);
                        measurable disease; ECOG PS 0-1; \>=1 prior
                        Tx

  **Key Exclusion**     Prior anti-PD-1/PD-L1; active autoimmune
                        disease; active IBD; systemic steroids \>10
                        mg/d

  **Key Phase 2a        ORR = 42.5% (17/40; 95% CI: 27.0%, 59.1%) ---
  Results (CSR)**       H0 REJECTED (p=0.011) \| Median DoR: NR \|
                        Median PFS: 8.3 months \| 12-mo OS: 79.8% \|
                        No treatment-related deaths
