**1. PURPOSE AND SCOPE**

This Batch Manufacturing Record (BMR) documents the manufacturing of
Cervimab Drug Product (DP) Batch CVM-DP-P1-001, a sterile,
preservative-free, concentrated solution of an anti-PD-1 monoclonal
antibody intended for intravenous infusion. This batch is manufactured
under current Good Manufacturing Practice (cGMP) conditions in
compliance with 21 CFR Parts 210, 211, and 600--680 for use in Phase 1
clinical studies.

The drug product is manufactured from Drug Substance (DS) Batch
CVM-DS-P1-001 (IgG4 anti-PD-1 monoclonal antibody, produced in CHO
cells). The DP formulation consists of 20 mg/mL Cervimab in a
histidine-buffered, sucrose-stabilized formulation with polysorbate 80.
